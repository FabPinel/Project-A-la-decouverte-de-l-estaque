Estaque is a charming suburban port with a village feel. Majestic villas, friendly bars, fish and seafood restaurants, and panisse stands (chickpea fritters) are set up by the sea. The port is full of boats, and the sandy and pebble beaches of the district offer a view of the city, against the backdrop of the limestone hills of the Estaque range. A simple market with regional food, clothing and crafts is held on Saturday mornings.
